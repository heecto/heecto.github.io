Coastal Carpet Pythons

Apperance

Coastal Carpet Pythons (Morelia Spilota Mcdowelli) is a large heavy-bodied snake that can grow to weigh 10kg and be as log as 3 meters in length, the average length of this snake is around 2.1 meters long (the longest recorded length was 4 meters). Adult Coastal Carpet Pythons are usually olive-green in colour and juveniles are ussually found in shades of brown. They are marked by pale and dark edged blotches, stripes or cross-bands and have a cream belly.

Habitat

Coastal Carpet Pythons normally frequent forest areas near water but also settle in rainforests, wet/dry fucalyptus forests, heathland, pastures and well-settled suburban/urban areas around Queensland and New South Wales
